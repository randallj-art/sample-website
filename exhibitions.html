EXHIBITIONS

In 2024, I showed a small selection of my work in a group show alongside my fellow senior art majors in the List Gallery at Swarthmore College. I was assigned the corner of the gallery with a blue-tinted window that revealed a staircase. How perfect! My peers and I learned how to select and arrange our works, how to hang paintings, quilts, and other kinds of artwork on walls, and how to share an exhibition space with other artists in harmony.
